## Saturday July 11 — Chosen Few Picnic 🏠
**This is the anchor.** The Chosen Few Old School Reunion Picnic, Jackson Park. 36th year. Literally the birthplace of house music, the DJs who invented it, all day in the park. For anyone who loves house music, this is a bucket-list day. Grab tickets early — it sells out.

- **Tickets:** [chosenfewdjs.com](https://chosenfewdjs.com) or Eventbrite
- All day event, starts noon-ish, goes into evening
- Casual, outdoor, summer picnic energy
- After the picnic → Smart Bar or keep it loose

## Friday July 10 — Club Night
**Smart Bar** (Lakeview, beneath Metro) — the definitive Chicago house club. This is where the music was born, and it's still where serious heads go. Check [Resident Advisor](https://ra.co) for who's playing July 10. Book ahead if there's a headliner.

**Spy Bar** (River North) — underground, packed, consistently good house programming. Good backup or second stop.

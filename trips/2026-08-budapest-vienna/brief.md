## Recommendation: Copenhagen + Malmö
**Why:** Denmark + Sweden = 2 new countries in one trip (Malmö is 35 min by train over the Øresund Bridge). Copenhagen is a fashion-forward, deeply walkable city — wife will feel stylish and look great there. Shopping on Kronprinsensgade and Klosterstræde is genuinely good: Ganni, Acne Studios, Stine Goya flagships + independent boutiques. Nyhavn and Frederiksberg Gardens = her best travel photos. Food scene is exceptional (post-Noma era). Gin cocktail bars are serious.

**Why not Dublin:** Weakest for shopping, food, and the "feel stylish" factor. Hard to recommend over the other two.

**Why Milan is also strong:** If she prioritizes luxury labels (Gucci, Prada, Bottega, LV) over new-country credit, the Quadrilatero della Moda and a Lake Como day are hard to beat. Italy not a new country though.

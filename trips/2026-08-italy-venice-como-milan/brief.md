## Intercity travel

## VENICE (Aug 11–13) — photogenic anchor
Arrive AM Aug 11, tired from overnight. Easy first day.
- **Stay:** boutique in San Marco / Dorsoduro / Cannaregio (mid-range boutique per budget style; water-view splurge optional for birthday trip).
- **Do:** wander, vaporetto down the Grand Canal, Dorsoduro + Cannaregio for non-touristy aperitivo, early-morning St. Mark's photos before crowds.
- **Her photos:** Grand Canal at golden hour, Rialto, a quiet canal in Cannaregio.
- **Dinner:** one nice seafood/cicchetti night. Reserve ~30 days out.

## LAKE COMO (Aug 13–15) — the lake + birthday eve
- **Stay base:** Como town (rail-convenient) or **Bellagio** (more romantic, photogenic — worth it for this trip). Varenna also strong + train-accessible.
- **Do:** lake ferry between Bellagio / Varenna / Menaggio, Villa del Balbianello or Villa Carlotta gardens (photogenic), lakeside aperitivo.
- **Gin/cocktail moment:** lakeside terrace bar at sunset — her vibe exactly.
- **Birthday eve (Aug 14):** lakeside dinner in Bellagio/Varenna.

## MILAN (Aug 15–18) — birthday + luxury shopping payoff
- **Stay:** Quadrilatero / Brera / city center on Marriott where possible (Explore rate).
- **Aug 15 🎂 Birthday dinner:** book a standout Milan table **now-ish (book ~45–60 days out = by ~July 1)**. Trendy upscale Italian or a rooftop with a serious cocktail/gin list.
- **Shopping:** **Quadrilatero della Moda** — Via Montenapoleone, Via della Spiga (Gucci, Prada, Bottega, LV, Loro Piana). This is the trip's luxury-shopping centerpiece for her.
- **Do:** Duomo + rooftop terraces, Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II, Brera wander, **Navigli aperitivo**, optional Last Supper (timed entry — book weeks ahead if wanted).
